About This Property

Whare Tui Lodge sits on the shores of Lake Wanaka at Glendhu Bay, with uninterrupted views of the Southern Alps and Mt Aspiring National Park. Built on a former sheep station dating to the 1870s, we transformed the land over two decades — planting over 8,000 trees including silver birch, kowhai, native flax, and toi-toi alongside the original beech forest.

The architecture draws on clean timber-and-glass design — floor-to-ceiling double-glazed windows frame the lake and mountains from every room. Six spacious suites each have en-suite bathrooms with underfloor heating, walk-in rain showers, and deep soaking tubs positioned with views of the ranges. The open-plan living area centres around a stone fireplace, with the chef's kitchen opening directly onto the outdoor entertaining deck.

This is our home. We converted it to exclusive-use because we believe the best way to experience Wanaka is with the whole lodge to yourself — not room-by-room in a hotel. Aroha handles the itineraries and local tips, I sort the logistics. No front desk, no corporate script. Just two Kiwis who know this place inside and out.
